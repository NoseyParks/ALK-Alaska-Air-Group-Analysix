# Alaska Air Group (ALK) Independent Analysis

## The 10-Sigma Anomaly: Undisclosed Cybersecurity Crisis and $120M Accounting Irregularities

**Published:** 15 December 2025  
**Author:** Tom Caton  

### Summary

This repository contains independent research documenting:

- A 10.40 standard deviation anomaly in Alaska Air Group's Q2 2025 loyalty programme issuance-to-revenue ratio
- $120M in undisclosed balance sheet reclassifications (Q3 2025)
- 265 verified cybersecurity incidents affecting Mileage Plan accounts at 24x peer airline rate
- Convergence between bottom-up fraud estimate ($43M) and excess partner redemptions ($45M)
